NHS number

An NHS number is a 10-digit number, for example, 485 777 3456. They can find it on any letter the NHS has sent them, on a prescription, or by logging in to a GP practice online service.

In Scotland, it's known as CHI number. In Northern Ireland, it's known as an H&C number.

It's OK if they do not have an NHS number or they do not live in the UK, they can still continue.
