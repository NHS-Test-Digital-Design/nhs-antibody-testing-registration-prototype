Follow these rules to take a good photo. Make sure:

  • Put your test strip against a plain background and position the camera so that all 4 corners of the test strip fit into the photo.
  • C should be at the top, followed by T, with the specimen well S at the bottom (see diagram).
  • If you want to make the photo clearer, move your camera closer to the test strip. Do not try to zoom or focus. We cannot accept photos that are blurred, dark, too far away or partly covered.
  • Make sure no personal information is in the photo, for example paperwork with your name, address, date of birth or bank details.
