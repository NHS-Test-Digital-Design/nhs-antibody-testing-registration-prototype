You must help the service by taking a clear photo, otherwise it may not be able to read your result.

  • Lay your test strip flat on a plain background. Do not use a white or shiny background.
  • Hold the smartphone or tablet flat above the test strip and move it until all 4 corners of the test strip fit into the outline on the screen.
  • Make sure the letters C and T on the test strip are the right way up. The specimen well (where you dropped the test fluid) must be at the bottom of the photo.
  • Make sure there are no shadows or reflections on the test strip.
  • Make sure no personal information is in the photo, for example paperwork with your name, address, date of birth or bank details.
