Use this service to share the result of your coronavirus test with the NHS and receive guidance about keeping yourself and others safe.

What you need to do

  • When your test kit arrives, carefully read the paper instructions sent with it
  • Then do your test by following the steps in the paper instructions
  • Finally use this service to share your test result

Sharing your result

When you share your result, you will receive guidance on any practical measures to take.

Sharing your result with the NHS will help the UK understand the spread of the virus.

Before you start

  • You will need your test kit's barcode number - it's printed on the box that the kit arrived in
