What you should know

You have told us that you believe your test is not void (sometimes called invalid).

The technology we use to read test results guarantees a high level of accurancy. For example, it is able to see faint lines on test strips which cannot be seen by the human eye.

This technology has read your result as a positive. To confirm whether you are infectious to others, please follow all the instructions below immediately.

What you need to do

Because your result was read as void, you should take another test immediately.

Keep following coronavirus advice including:

  • regular handwashing
  • social distancing
  • wearing a face covering where recommended

You only need to self-isolate if:

  • you get symptoms of coronavirus
  • someone you live with tests positive, or has symptoms and has not been tested yet
  • you've been traced as a contact of someone who tested positive
