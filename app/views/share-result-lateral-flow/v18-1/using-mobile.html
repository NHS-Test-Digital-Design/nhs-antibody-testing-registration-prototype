Find out how this service works

When you take a photo of the test strip, the test result is automatically read for you using NHS approved machine learning technology.

It’s more reliable and accurate than manually reading a result from the test strip, and helps you to confirm the test result.

By taking a photo, you agree to use the Automated Decision Making (ADM) service.

If you prefer not to take a photo, you can enter the test result manually.
