Register here before you take a rapid coronavirus (COVID-19) test at a test site. You can register yourself, or someone else who is taking a test.

Why register

Registering at home beforehand will save you time, so you do not have to do this at the test site.

Your answers will be saved on a test pass, which you bring to your appointment. Staff will scan your pass and instantly know who you are.

This is the quickest and easiest way to get yourself tested.

What you’ll need:

  • a mobile number (for you, or for the person you're registering)

How we use your data

To find out how we use your personal data when you register for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note.
