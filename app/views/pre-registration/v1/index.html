Register for a rapid coronavirus (COVID-19) test at a test site. You can do this for yourself or someone else.

Registering will create a test pass, which you take to your appointment. Someone at the test site will scan in your pass, which will link you to the swab test kit you're given. It means you can then receive your test results. You must register and create your pass within 24 hours of your appointment.

What you’ll need:

  • to have an appointment at a symptoms-free test site (you'll have booked this through your local council)
  • a mobile number (for you, or for the person you're getting a test for)

How we use your data

To find out how we use your personal data when you register for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note.
