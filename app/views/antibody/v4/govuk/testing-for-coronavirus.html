If you currently have symptoms of coronavirus, you can get a free test to check if you currently have coronavirus (COVID-19). Find out more about coronavirus symptoms on the NHS website.

There's also a test to check if you’ve had coronavirus before, but it's not widely available yet.

Testing to check if you currently have coronavirus

This test is sometimes known as a 'swab' test.

There is a test for:

  • anyone who currently has coronavirus symptoms - if you live in Leicester and do not currently have symptoms, you can also take this test
  • care home residents or staff members - they’re still eligible for the test even if they do not currently have symptoms

Testing to check if you've had coronavirus before

Care workers, check to see if you have had coronavirus before

Even if a test shows you have coronavirus antibodies, you might not be immune from catching it again.

You can buy these tests privately. You’ll need to pay. They may not always be accurate.

More about antibody testing.

Stopping the virus from spreading

To stop the virus from spreading, the NHS has a service ('test and trace') that:

  • tests anyone with coronavirus symptoms quickly to find out if they have the virus
  • finds people who’ve been in contact with anyone who tests positive and tells them to self-isolate if they need to
