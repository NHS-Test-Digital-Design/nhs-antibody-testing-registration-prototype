About self-test kits

Self-test kits are fingerprick blood tests, they:

  • are free and take 5 days to arrive in the post
  • are approved by the NHS as reliable and accurate
  • come with everything you need to test yourself at home and get your result in minutes

With a self-test kit, you do not need to post a blood sample to get a result.
