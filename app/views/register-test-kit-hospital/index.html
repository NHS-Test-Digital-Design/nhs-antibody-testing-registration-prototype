Register your coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR test kit so we can send your test results to you.

Check what you need

To register your home test kit you'll need:

  • your home test kit
  • your order ID (it's in the text message we sent you)

What you need to do next

4 days before your hospital appointment:

  • contact the courier before 4pm to book next day collection of your test kit

3 days before your hospital appointment:

  • take your test by 7am on the morning of your courier collection
  • give it to the courier (they will arrive between 8am and 4pm)

Get help to register the test

To have a live video call with trained staff, download the free Be My Eyes app for iOS or for Android and go to the 'specialised help' section.

How we use your data

To find out how we use your personal data when you register for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note.
