Use this service to order a home test for patients who have a hospital appointment for medical procedure.

When to order a home test for your patients

You should request tests for your patients no sooner than 9 days and no later than 7 days of their appointment date. This is so there is enough time for their test to be delivered and completed.

There are 2 ways you can order a test:

  • Use our spreadsheet to order tests for up to 50 patients
  • Add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You will need the following patient details to request a test:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • Delivery address
  • Date of procedure
