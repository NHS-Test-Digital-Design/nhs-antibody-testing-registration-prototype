Use this service to arrange a test pass for drivers and crew entering the UK. It takes a few minutes to register and will make the process faster and easier at the coronavirus (COVID-19) test site.

Who can use this service

Anyone arriving in the UK in heavy goods vehicles, vans or light goods vehicles must take a COVID-19 test. You can only use this service if you or the people you are registering are driving a heavy goods vehicle, van or light goods vehicle into the UK.
