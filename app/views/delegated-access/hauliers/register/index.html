If you are arriving in the UK and drive a heavy goods vehicle, van or light goods vehicle, you must take a COVID-19 test. This applies to drivers and crew of both UK-based and non-UK hauliers.

This service is also available in Polskie, Română, български, čeština, Dutch, Français, Deutsche, Magyar, Lietuvis, русский, Español, Türk, Cymraeg

Why you should register

On arrival in the UK, hauliers are required to do a test. Registering now only takes a few minutes and will ensure a smooth process when you arrive at the test site.

Who can use this service

You can only use this service if you or the person you are registering are driving a heavy goods vehicle, van or light goods vehicle into the UK.
