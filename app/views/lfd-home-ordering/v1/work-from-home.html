What is a support bubble?
Support bubble

If you live alone or you’re a single parent who lives alone with your children, you can meet with 1 other household and form a support bubble. You can think of yourselves as a "household".

More about support bubbles

Childcare bubble

If you live in a household with anyone aged under 14, you can form a ‘childcare bubble’. This allows friends or family from one other household to provide informal childcare.
