Use this service to register coronavirus (COVID-19) tests for your organisation.

Before you start

  • Read of all the instructions in your guidance pack
  • You must have already completed your testing (on the day of your courier booking)
  • You must have already booked a courer via the courier portal
  • If you are bulk uploading test kits to register and have pre-populated the Excel template, you should have this ready. Make sure that your file matches the latest version of the Excel template before you upload

What you'll need to register tests

  • The barcode for each test kit you are registering
  • The email address and mobile phone number to send the test results to
  • The permission of people who have been tested to enter their personal details

If you need help

First, check your guidance pack for instructions. You can also read the Frequently asked questions.

How we use your data

To find out how we use your personal data when you register for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note.
