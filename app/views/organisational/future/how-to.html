Organisation accounts allows organisation managers to distribute coronavirus tests directly to staff's homes and to monitor staff's testing compliance.

Setting up your account

  1. Register for an account using your unique organisation number (UON) and orgnanistaion details.
  2. Check your organisation details.
  3. Check your organisation testing requirements.
  4. Read the online guidance pack.
  5. Invite your staff to create personal testing accounts.
  6. Review or enter your staff testing schedules based on their working days.
  7. Monitor your staff's testing compliance.
  8. If necessary, act on positive test results.
