Use this service to register coronavirus (COVID-19) tests for your organisation.

Before you start

  • Read the online guidance pack and your printed instructions
  • book your courier portal here
  • take your test on the day of your courier collection
  • make sure the people getting a test are happy for you to enter their personal details

What you'll need

  • a completed and updated version of our spreadsheet template if you’re registering lots of tests
  • the barcode or unique reference number for each test kit you’re registering
  • the email and mobile where you can get the test results

If you need help

Check the guidance pack for full instructions or see our frequently asked questions.

How we use personal data

To find out how we use people’s data when you register them for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note.
