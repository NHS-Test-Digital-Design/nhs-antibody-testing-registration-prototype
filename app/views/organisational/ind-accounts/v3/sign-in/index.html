If you've been given coronavirus (COVID-19) test kits by an organisation, use this service to register or report test results for yourself or other people in the organisation.

This service supports people who work in:

  • Health care
  • Care settings
  • Education
  • Prison service

We will link your test to the organisation that gave you the test kits. If an organisation did not give you these test kits, or is not one of the areas listed, this is not the correct service to use.

What you need

  • Name and postcode of organisation
  • Test kit barcode
  • An email address
