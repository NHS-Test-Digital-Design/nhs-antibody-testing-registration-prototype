If you have been given a coronavirus (COVID-19) test by an organisation, you can register and report your test results using this service.

This service supports people who work in:

  • Health care
    Independent health providers such as dentists
  • Care settings
    Care homes, supported living and domiciliary workers
  • Education
    Nurseries, schools, colleges and universities
  • Prison service
    Prisons and Ministry of Justice

We will link your test to your organisation. If your organisation did not give you a test, this is not the correct service to use.

What you need

  • Name and postcode of the organisation that gave you the test
  • Test kit barcode
  • Email address

If you are a domiciliary worker, you can use a NHS log in to register your test result. Sign in or create an account.
