If you have been given a coronavirus (COVID-19) test kits by an organisation, you can register and report your test results using this service.

This service supports people who work in:

  • Health care
    Independent health providers such as dentists
  • Care settings
    Care homes, supported living and domiciliary workers
  • Education
    Nurseries, schools, colleges and universities
  • Prison service
    Prisons and Ministry of Justice

We will link your test to the organisation that gave you the test kits. If an organisation did not give you these test kits, this is not the correct service to use.

What you need

  • Name and postcode of organisation
  • Test kit barcode
  • An email address

If you are a domiciliary worker, you can use a NHS log in to register your test result.
