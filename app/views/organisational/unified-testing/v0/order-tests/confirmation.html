What happens next

If your order was successful, we'll send your coronavirus test kit to the test site.

The test kits contains a swab, instructions on how to use it, and return packaging.

It should arrive at the address you gave us in the next 48 hours.

You'll receive email updates on the delivery status of your test kits.

If your order was not successful, we'll send you an email with what to do next.

Once you get your test kits

Follow the instructions in the test kits when they arrive.

The instructions will tell you:

  • how to do the swab test
  • when to do the swab test
  • how to return your swab sample

Need help?

If you have any problems, call the customer contact centre:

  • England, Wales and Northern Ireland: call 119 (free from mobile and landlines)
  • Scotland: call 0300 303 2713 (charged at your standard network rate)

This helpline is open from 7am to 11pm.

If you're worried

If your condition gets worse, or you do not get better after 7 days, use the NHS 111 online coronavirus service. Only call 111 if you cannot get help online. For a medical emergency, dial 999.
