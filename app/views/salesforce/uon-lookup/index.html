You can lookup your Unique Organisation Number (UON) using your existing organisation ID and postcode.

  • Care homes in England: enter your CQC location ID (it looks like this: '1-123456789', note this is different to your CQC provider ID, which is a similar format)
  • Care homes in Wales: enter your Care Inspectorate Wales Number (it looks like this: 'SIN-12345678-abcd')
  • Care homes in Northern Ireland: enter your RQIA number (it looks like this: '1234')
  • Care homes in Scotland: enter your Care Inspectorate number (it looks like this: 'CS1234567890')
  • GP surgeries: enter your ODS code (it looks like this: ‘A12345’)
  • Dental surgeries: enter your NHS location ID (it looks like this: ‘A123B’)
  • Schools, school-based nurseries, maintained nurseries or further education providers in England: enter your DfE URN or UKPRN (it looks like this: ‘123456’ or this: ‘12345678’)
  • Private, voluntary and independent nurseries in England: enter your Ofsted URN (it looks like this: ‘EY123456’). If your URN does not start with 'EY', please add it before the numbers like this 'EY123456'

If you are unsure of your organisation number please call the contact centre:

  • England, Wales and Northern Ireland: call 119 (free from mobiles and landlines)
  • Scotland: call 0300 303 2713 (charged at your standard network rate)

Lines are open 7am to 11pm.
