It’s important to take your test 3 days before your procedure, so it can go ahead as planned.

You need to take your test on 2 July, by 7am at the latest, so it’s ready for courier collection between 8am and 4pm.

If you've already done the test, tell us the date and time you did it. We need to know this so the lab can work out when the test is due to expire.

If you take your test on a different date than 2 July, you’ll need to call the hospital as this may affect your procedure.
