Use this service to register coronavirus (COVID-19) test kits for:

  • care home residents and staff
  • people tested at their GP or dental surgery

after they take the swab test.

If a member of staff gets coronavirus symptoms, send them home immediately to self isolate. Then they'll need to apply for a coronavirus test here.

To complete this form, you'll need

  • the test kit you are registering
  • your mobile number, or a mobile number for the person you're registering it for
  • the permission of the person being tested to enter their personal details
