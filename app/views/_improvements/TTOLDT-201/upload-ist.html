The main symptoms of coronavirus have been updated.

View the latest coronavirus symptoms and self-isolation guidance

The main symptoms of coronavirus are:

  • a high temperature
  • a new, continuous cough
  • a loss or change to your sense of smell or taste
  • a new headache
  • feeling more tired or exhausted than usual
  • new muscle aches
  • a loss of appetite
! Warning If they have a severe headache or they're worried about symptoms, contact NHS 111.

When to self-isolate

People who have any of the following symptoms (and anyone they live with) should self-isolate while waiting for their result:

  • a high temperature
  • a new, continuous cough
  • a loss or change to their sense of smell or taste

They should only leave self-isolation to get or post a test.

People with the other coronavirus symptoms may not need to self-isolate. Read more about when to self-isolate and what to do.
