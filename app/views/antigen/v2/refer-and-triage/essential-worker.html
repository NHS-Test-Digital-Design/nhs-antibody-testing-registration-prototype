Priority groups help to make sure people who need tests the most get them first.
In England
Essential workers(opens in new tab) such as NHS and social care staff, teachers and transport staff are in a priority group.
In Scotland
Essential workers, those on the Highest Risk List or eligible for new COVID-19 treatments are in a priority group.
In Wales
People who have been told they are eligible for new Covid-19 treatments(opens in new tab) are in a priority group.
In Northern Ireland
Essential workers, or those eligible for new Covid-19 treatments(opens in new tab) are in a priority group.
