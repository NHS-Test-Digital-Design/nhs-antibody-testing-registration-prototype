We want to know if the person is registered as male or female on their GP record. We need this, and other details you gave us, to find the person’s GP record and save their test result there.
Why aren't there more options?
At the moment GP records only say if a person is ‘male’ or ‘female’.

If the person does not identify as either male or female, please tell us their sex registered at birth (written on their birth certificate).

If the person has had gender surgery and updated their GP record, please tell us the gender they registered.

We’re sorry this question is not inclusive. We want to improve the service in the future so it works for everyone.
