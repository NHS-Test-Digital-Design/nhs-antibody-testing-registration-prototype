We need to know which gender is recorded on the person's GP record so we can save their test result there.
Why aren't there more options?
At the moment GP records are not inclusive. They only say if a person is 'man' or 'woman'.

If the person does not identify as either, please tell us their sex registered at birth (written on their birth certificate).

If the person has had gender surgery and updated their GP record, please tell us the gender they registered.

We’re sorry this question is not inclusive. We want to improve the service in the future so it works for everyone.
