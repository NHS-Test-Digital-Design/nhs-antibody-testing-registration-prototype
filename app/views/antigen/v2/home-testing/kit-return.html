Before you register your test kit, it's very important that you follow the paper instructions on how to return it.

If possible, post your test kit at your nearest Royal Mail priority postbox. If you cannot get to a priority postbox, read the paper instructions on how to book a courier.

To check where your nearest Royal Mail priority postbox is:

  • visit www.royalmail.com/services-near-you
  • click the 'Show my nearest priority postboxes' tickbox
  • enter the postcode of where you're staying and select the address
