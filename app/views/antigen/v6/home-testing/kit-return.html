! Warning Before you register your test kit, it is very important that you know how to return it, and that you have both your Royal Mail and test kit barcodes.

The Royal Mail barcode is different from your home test kit barcode. If you don't have it, you will need to register a different way.

If possible, post your test kit at your nearest Royal Mail priority postbox.

If you cannot get to a priority postbox, read the paper instructions on how to book a courier.
