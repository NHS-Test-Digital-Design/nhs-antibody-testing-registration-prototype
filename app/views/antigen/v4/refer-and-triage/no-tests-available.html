What to do if you have symptoms

If you have symptoms, self-isolate straight away until you get your results. Do not leave home except to post a test kit or for a PCR test appointment.

Check if people you live with need to self-isolate on NHS.UK

If your symptoms get worse or you need medical assistance:

  • Use 111 online
  • Call 111 if you're in England, Wales and Scotland
  • Call a GP or GP out-of-hours service if you're in Northern Ireland
  • Call 999 if you feel very unwell or think there's something seriously wrong
