A coronavirus testing account lets you save your details, so you do not need to enter them each time you register a test.

Your testing account uses NHS login. This makes it easier and quicker for you to securely access online health and care services with one username and password.

To register your coronavirus test, you can:

  • use your existing NHS login details, if you have them
  • create your NHS login using your email address and UK mobile phone number
  • continue without using NHS login

You only need to create your NHS account once.
