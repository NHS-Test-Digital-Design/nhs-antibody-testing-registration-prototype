Use this service to order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you’re travelling to England from outside the UK.

You need to do this at least X days before you arrive in England.

A travel package of 2 tests costs £210.

Who this service is for

You can order tests for yourself and 3 other people you’re travelling with, if they’re staying in quarantine at the same address as you.

What you need before you start

  • quarantine address in England
  • passport number
  • flight, ferry or Eurostar number
  • payment card
Start now

About your quarantine

If you enter England from an ‘amber’ country, you must quarantine for 10 days at a location of your choice.

During your quarantine, you must take a coronavirus test on day 2 and day 8 (where your day of arrival in the UK is day 0).

We’ll send the test kits to the address where you’ll stay in quarantine. You should receive them in time to take your day 2 test.

Passenger locator form (PLF)

You must complete this form online before you arrive in the UK: https://www.gov.uk/provide-journey-contact-details-before-travel-uk

About the tests

You’ll take a coronavirus PCR test, which is sent to a lab to be checked. The test involves taking a swab of the inside of your nose and the back of your throat, using a long cotton bud.
