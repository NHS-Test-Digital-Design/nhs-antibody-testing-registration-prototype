In Northern Ireland, you can order rapid lateral flow tests if:

  • a doctor or specialist has said you're eligible for COVID-19 treatments (opens in new tab) and you should order tests in case you get symptoms
  • your GP or healthcare professional has asked you to take a test
  • your doctor or healthcare professional has told you to get a test because you're being admitted to hospital or having a hospital procedure

You can also order rapid lateral flow tests if (the following links open in a new tab):

  • you are living in, working in or visiting high risk or vulnerable settings, for example care homes and hospitals
  • you have symptoms of COVID-19
  • you have COVID-19 and want to check if your test result is still positive after day 5. Find out how long you should isolate if you test positive for COVID-19
  • you’re a close contact of someone who has tested positive for COVID-19

If you are not eligible for tests

If you have symptoms, you should book a PCR test online or call 119.

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19. If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, speak to your GP or contact 111. In an emergency dial 999.

You can also find advice and support if you're worried about COVID-19.
