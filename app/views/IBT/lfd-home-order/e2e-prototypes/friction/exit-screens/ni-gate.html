In Northern Ireland, you can order rapid lateral flow tests if:

  • a doctor or specialist has said you're eligible for COVID-19 treatments (opens in new tab) and you should order tests in case you get symptoms
  • your GP or healthcare professional has asked you to take a test
  • your doctor or healthcare professional has told you to get a test because you're being admitted to hospital or having a hospital procedure

You can also order rapid lateral flow tests if (the following links open in a new tab):

  • you are living in, working in or visiting high risk or vulnerable settings(opens in new tab), for example care homes and hospitals
  • you have symptoms of COVID-19(opens in new tab)
  • you have COVID-19 and want to check if your test result is still positive after day 5. Find out how long you should isolate if you test positive for COVID-19(opens in new tab)
  • you’re a close contact(opens in new tab) of someone who has tested positive for COVID-19

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19 (opens in new tab). If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, contact your GP or your GP out of hours service. In an emergency dial 999.
