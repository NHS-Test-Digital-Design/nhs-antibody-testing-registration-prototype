Most people in England cannot get free tests any more.

However, you may still be able to get free tests:

  • you're eligible for COVID-19 treatments, like antiviral medicines
  • a healthcare professional has recently asked you to get a test
  • you work in the NHS, or for an independent healthcare provider (IHP), or in adult social care
  • you're going into hospital for a procedure or treatment
