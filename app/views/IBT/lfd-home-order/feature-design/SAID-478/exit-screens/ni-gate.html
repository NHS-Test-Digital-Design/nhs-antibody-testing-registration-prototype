In Northern Ireland, you can order rapid lateral flow tests if:

  • a doctor or specialist has said you may be eligible for COVID-19 treatments(opens in new tab) and you should order tests in case you get symptoms
  • your GP or healthcare professional has asked you to take a test

You can also order rapid lateral flow tests if you:

  • are living in, working in or visiting high risk or vulnerable settings, for example care homes and hospital environments
  • have symptoms of COVID-19(opens in new tab)
  • have COVID-19 and want to check if your test result is still positive after day 5. Find out how long you should isolate if you test positive for COVID-19(opens in new tab)

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19 (opens in new tab). If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, contact your GP or your GP out of hours service. In an emergency dial 999.
