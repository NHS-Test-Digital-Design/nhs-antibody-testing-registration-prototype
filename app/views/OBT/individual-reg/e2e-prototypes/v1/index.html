Use this service to register or report coronavirus (COVID-19) test kits for yourself or other people in your organisation.

We'll share your registration with the organisation that gave you the test kits.

If an organisation did not give you these test kits, this is not the correct service to use.

Select your organisation type for more information

Adult social care organisations

Organisations include:

  • care homes
  • domiciliary care
  • adult day care centres
  • exta care housing
  • supported living
  • hospice
  • independent health providers

  • register PCR tests for yourself or other people

Before you start

  • if you need to, book your courier collection here
  • if you're doing a PCR test, take it on the same day you're returning the test kits
  • if you're registering test kits for other people, make sure they have freely given you consent to register them. Their results will be sent to the contact details you enter

What you'll need

  • your UON or organisation name or postcode
  • test kit barcode(s)
  • an email address

How we use personal data

  • To find out how we use people’s data when you register them for a COVID-19 test, read the COVID-19 privacy note.
