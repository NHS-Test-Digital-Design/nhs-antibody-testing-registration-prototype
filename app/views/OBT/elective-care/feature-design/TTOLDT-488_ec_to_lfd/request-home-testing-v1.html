Order rapid lateral flow test kits for your elective care patients

Use this service for patients who have a hospital appointment for a procedure.

When to order test kits

Order test kits for your patients 6 to 11 days before their hospital procedure. This allows enough time for them to take a test and report their test result.

There are 2 ways you can order test kits:

  • use our spreadsheet to order test kits for up to 100 patients at a time
  • add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You will need the following patient details to request test kits:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • delivery address

If you do not have all these details or your patient cannot take a rapid lateral flow test, you’ll need to arrange a different way to get a test. Find out what a rapid lateral flow test involves (opens in new tab).

Get test results

! Warning You will no longer get an email with the patient's test result.
  • patients must take a rapid lateral flow test and report their result online. If they cannot use the online service, they can call 119
  • they will get a text to confirm their reported result which they can bring to the procedure to prove they are negative
  • patients will call the trust if they are positive
  • it is up to the trust to explain all of this to patients

How we use patient data

To find out how we use patient’s personal data, read the COVID-19 privacy policy (opens in new tab).
