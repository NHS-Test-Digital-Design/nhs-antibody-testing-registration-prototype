Order a home LFD test for your elective care patients

Use this service for patients who have a hospital appointment for a procedure.

! Warning Only order tests for patients who do not have coronavirus symptoms

When to order a home test

Order tests for your patients 6 to 11 days before their hospital procedure. This allows enough time for them to take and return their test kit.

There are 2 ways you can order a test:

  • use our spreadsheet to order tests for up to 100 patients at a time
  • add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You will need the following patient details to request a test:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • delivery address

If you do not have all these details or your patient cannot take a home test, you’ll need to arrange a different way to get a test. Find out what a home test involves (opens in new tab).

Guidance manual

For more information read our guidance manual.

Get test results

! Warning Patients must self-report their results

With the recent changes in elective care patient testing, you will no longer receive an email with the patient's test result.

Patients must use the LFD self-report service to register their result. After they do this, they will receive an SMS confirming their result. They can bring this to the procedure to prove they are negative.

How we use patient data

To find out how we use patient’s personal data, read the coronavirus privacy note (opens in new tab).
