Order PCR test kits for your elective care patients

Use this service for patients who have a hospital appointment for a procedure.

When to order PCR test kits

Order test kits for your patients 6 to 11 days before their hospital procedure. This allows enough time for them to take and return their PCR test kit.

There are 2 ways you can order PCR test kits:

  • use our PCR test kit spreadsheet to order for up to 100 patients at a time
  • add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You will need the following patient details to request a PCR test kit:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • delivery address

If you do not have all these details or your patient cannot take a PCR test, you’ll need to arrange a different way to get a test. Find out what a PCR test involves (opens in new tab).

Get PCR test results

We'll send the results to you by email. Patients will get their results by text.

How we use patient data

To find out how we use patient’s personal data, read the COVID-19 privacy policy (opens in new tab).
