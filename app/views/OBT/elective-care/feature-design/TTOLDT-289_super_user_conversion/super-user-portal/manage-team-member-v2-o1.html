    There are 2 ways for team members to sign in:

    Email and password

    • you can create team members with an email and password
    • team members cannot view test results

    Smartcard

    • you cannot create team members who have a Smartcard
    • team members need to ask for access when they first use their Smartcard on the test kit ordering portal
    • a team member with enhanced access can go in and approve this request
    • team members can view test results

    Permissions

    Team member has enhanced permissions. To change these permissions, contact the elective care support team.

    ElectiveCareTesting@dhsc.gov.uk
    Learn about team member permissions
    Depending on a team member's role, they will be given certain permissions. The available permissions are:

    Standard

    Order home tests, check patient details, view test results (when signed in with a Smartcard) and confirm procedure cancellations.

    Enhanced

    Standard permissions, plus add team members and manage groups.

    Account status
