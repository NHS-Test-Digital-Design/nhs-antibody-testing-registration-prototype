What to do if they have or develop symptoms

In England

They should stay at home and avoid contact with other people until they get their test result.
Find out more about when to stay at home and what to do (opens in new tab).

In Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland

! Warning If they have symptoms, they must self-isolate straight away until they get their result. They must not leave home, except to post a test kit or for a PCR test appointment.

Find out more about self-isolation and treating symptoms. The following links open in a new tab:

  • Scotland: Self-isolation guidance
  • Wales: Self-isolation guidance
  • Northern Ireland: Self-isolation guidance
