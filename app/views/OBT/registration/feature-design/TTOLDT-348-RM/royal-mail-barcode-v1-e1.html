The Royal Mail barcode reference is on the right-hand side of the postage label that goes on the return box. Do not enter or scan the QR code, which is on the left-hand side of the label.

You’ll need to enter this information if you have any questions about the status of your test kit.

Entering the Royal Mail barcode reference also helps the labs process test kits quicker.

Choose one of these ways to enter the barcode reference:

  • use the camera on your phone or computer
  • use your handheld scanner
  • enter it manually
How to use the handheld barcode scanner
  1. Click in the ‘Royal Mail barcode reference’ box.
  2. Scan the barcode using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box.
  3. Click in the ‘Confirm Royal Mail barcode reference’ box.
  4. Scan the barcode again using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box.
  5. Continue.
