Use this service to register or report coronavirus (COVID-19) test kits for yourself or other people

We will link your test to the organisation that gave you the test kits. If an organisation did not give you these test kits, this is not the correct service to use.

Select your organisation type for more information

Domiciliary care

Use this service to:

  • register PCR tests for yourself or other people
  • register and report lateral flow tests resutls for yourself or other people

Use an account to:

  • register or report your own test kits
  • save your personal details to make registration faster
  • your personal and account details will not be shared with your organisation
  • your test kit registration will be shared with your organisation by not your test results or personal details
  • if you have other people to your account, only the lead account members details will be accessed
  • if you do not already have an NHS account, you can create one by using this service

Before you start

  • if you need to, book your courier collection here
  • take PCR tests on the same day you're returning the kits
  • make sure people getting tested have freely given you consent to register them, and get their results sent to the contact details you enter

What you'll need

  • your UON or organisation name or postcode
  • test kit barcode(s)
  • an email address
