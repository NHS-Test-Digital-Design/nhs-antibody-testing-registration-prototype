Upload test details

This spreadsheet allows you to register both PCR and lateral flow tests. You can also report lateral flow test results.

It is designed to save your organisation time and effort by uploading multiple test details at once.

To do this:

  • download our spreadsheet if you have not already
  • remove any people who have not been tested
  • do not add any sheets, row or columns
  • enter details for up to 100 people at a time
  • save the completed spreadsheet and then upload it

You must complete the spreadsheet in the same language you have used to register tests.

For help, see multiple registration upload guidance (opens in new tab).
