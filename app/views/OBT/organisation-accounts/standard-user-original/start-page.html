Important

PCR test registrations will not be processed unless they are for people who are having COVID-19 treatments.

Use this service to register coronavirus (COVID-19) tests for your organisation.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

Before you start

  • read the online guidance pack (opens in new tab) and your printed instructions
  • take tests on the same day you're returning the kits

Get consent

Make sure the people getting tested have given you consent to:

  • register their tests
  • get their results sent to the contact details you enter

What you'll need

  • your 8 digit organisation number
  • if you're registering lots of tests, use the correct spreadsheet (if you don't have this, you can download it during the registration process)
  • you'll need to record the details of staff and non-staff on separate spreadsheets
  • the barcode for each test
  • the email address (and ideally mobile number) to get the test results

How we use personal data

To find out how we use your personal data when you register them for a coronavirus test, read the coronavirus privacy note (opens in new tab).
