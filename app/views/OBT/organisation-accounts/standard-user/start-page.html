Use this service to:

  • register PCR tests
  • register lateral flow tests
  • report lateral flow test results
This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

Before you start

  • read the online guidance pack and your printed instructions
  • if you need to, book your courier collection here
  • take tests on the same day you're returning the kits

Get consent

Make sure the people you've tested have given you consent to:

  • register their tests
  • get their results sent to the contact details you enter

To find out how we use people’s data, read Coronavirus (COVID-19) testing: privacy information

What you'll need

  • your 8 digit unique organisation number (UON)
  • the barcode for each test
  • the email address (and ideally mobile number) to get the test results

If you're registering lots of tests

You can use this spreadsheet to add up to 100 tests at a time . Record the details of staff and non-staff on separate spreadsheets.
