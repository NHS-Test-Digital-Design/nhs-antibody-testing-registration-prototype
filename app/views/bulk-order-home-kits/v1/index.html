Use this service for members of your organisation who need to be tested as part of their role

When to order a home test

Order tests for your members x days before their test date. This allows enough time for them to receive and take their test.

There are 2 ways you can order a test:

  • use our spreadsheet to order tests for up to x members at a time
  • add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You'll need the following patient details to request a test:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • delivery address

If you do not have all these details or your patient cannot take a home test, you'll need to arrange a different way to get a test. Find out what a home test involves involves.
