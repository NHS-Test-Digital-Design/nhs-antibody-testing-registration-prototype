Government accessibility requirements

  • Meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines

    Met

    The service meets level AA of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 as a minimum.

  • Have an accessibility statement

    In progress

    The service has published an accessibility statement that follows the standard set out by Government Digital Services.

  • Test with assistive technology

    Met

    The service must be tested with commonly used assistive technologies and any issues fixed.

  • Include people with access needs in research

    Not met

    The service needs to do user research with people who have a range of access needs.

Inclusion requirements

  • End-to-end usability testing

    Met

    The live service is regularly tested from beginning to end to see how usable it is.

  • Exclusion mapping

    In progress

    An exclusion mapping workshop has taken place to identify inclusion needs.
